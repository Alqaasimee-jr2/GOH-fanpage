Jin Mori is the main protagonist in "The God of High School," a South Korean webtoon series created by Yongje Park. His character has a rich and complex background.

Background: Jin Mori is initially introduced as a high school student living a seemingly ordinary life. However, his true identity is anything but ordinary. Unbeknownst to him, he is the descendant of the legendary martial artist Jin Tae-Jin, who is also known as the Jade Emperor.

The God of High School Tournament: Jin Mori's journey truly begins when he enters "The God of High School" tournament, a competition that seeks to determine the strongest high school student in South Korea. As he participates in the tournament, he quickly gains attention for his exceptional martial arts skills, particularly in Renewal Taekwondo.

Revelations and Monkey King's Power: As the series progresses, it is revealed that Jin Mori is the host for the powerful being known as the Monkey King. The Monkey King lends him extraordinary abilities, including the power to summon the Ruyi Jingu Bang, a magical staff associated with Chinese mythology.

Charyeok and Charyeok Borrowing: Jin Mori's connection with the Monkey King allows him to tap into Charyeok, a power that involves borrowing strength from divine beings. The Monkey King's influence enhances Mori's combat abilities, making him a formidable force in the tournament.

Personal Growth and Challenges: Throughout the series, Jin Mori faces numerous challenges, both in the tournament and in his personal life. He encounters powerful opponents, uncovers the mysteries of his family's past, and experiences significant character development. His journey is not just about physical strength but also about self-discovery and understanding his role in the larger supernatural conflicts unfolding in the story.
